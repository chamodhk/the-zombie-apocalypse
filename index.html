How to Play

Click "New Game" for a new game.
In "Choose Your Character" you can choose your character.
In "Choose Your Level" you can choose your level.
Press Space to Jump over zombies and get points.
If you get hit by a zombie, you lose.
If you get 1500 points, you win.
Click "Back" to go back to the main menu.
